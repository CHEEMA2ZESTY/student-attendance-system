About This Project

This Student Attendance System is designed for students and instructors to efficiently track and manage attendance.

It includes features such as attendance marking, real-time records management, search and filter functionality, progress tracking, and a dark/light mode for better usability.

About the Developer

Hi, I'm Chima, a Computer Science student at LASU with a passion for building practical and user-friendly applications. This project is part of my SIWES IT training, aimed at enhancing attendance management for students and instructors.

How It Works

The Student Attendance System allows students to mark their attendance quickly and accurately. Instructors can view records, filter attendance, and track progress over time.

  • Enter your name and select your attendance status (Present, Late, or Absent).
  • Records are saved automatically and can be edited if needed.
  • Search and filter options help in locating specific records.
  • Progress tracking gives an overview of attendance statistics.

Future Improvements

Planned updates for this project include:

  • Integration with student portals for authentication.
  • Automated email notifications for attendance updates.
  • Export options for attendance records in CSV or PDF format.
  • Improved UI and accessibility features.
